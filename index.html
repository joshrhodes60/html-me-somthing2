The Mountains Are Calling

Denver

My partner and I started off the trip by flying into Denver. There we got to go visit the Rockies baseball stadium and went sight seeing at the historic 16th street mall.

Golden

"In Golden we spent most of the day visiting the Coors light brewery. The brewery tour was really good, you got to see how the different beers were made and even got some free ones after the tour. Definetly a must if you are ever in that area."

Morrison

One of my favorite things of the trip was visiting Morrison. There we went to go visit the red rock ampitheatre which is amazing.Although there wasn't a concert at the time I could only imagine how cool that would be.

Estes Park

For the last day of the trip we made our way to Estes Park. There we started off by visiting the Stanley Hotel where the shining and some other films were made.The whole property of the hotel and seeing where the different scenes from movies were taken and the history of the place was pretty amazing. Lastly we went on a 16 mile hike into the mountains to an amazing view as you will see above.
